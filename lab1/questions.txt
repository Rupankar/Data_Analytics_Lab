Assignment -1

This lab includes questions that will help you to acquaint with the basic programming skills in R. 
The questions includes the application of branching, looping as well as the functions.


1. WAP in R to find the highest of three numbers.

2. WAP in R to find display the sum of n natural numbers using loop.

3. Write the above two programs in R using functions.

4. WAP in R to find the multiplication table (from 1 to 10)

5. WAP in R to find the sum of n natural numbers without formula.

6. Design a simple calculator in R.
